What to do in the beginning?

Beginnings are always the hardest.

First find some gravel, dig it to get flint. After you found flint, you need to break it. I recommend that you get at least 3 pieces of flint.


Craft a flint knife, from here you need to collect string, then make flint axe and pickaxe, you need to collect some wood.


It is reccomended to cut down some birch trees, so you can craft paper, and paper is needed to craft matches. Dark in RLCraft is one of your biggest enemies.
